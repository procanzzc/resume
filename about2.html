路 科 简 介 / ABOUT LOOK

深圳市路科创意设计公司创立于2011年，隶属深圳市利得来集团（深圳宝安100强深圳市重点民营企业）的下属子公司。 经过五年的辛酸历程，已成为中国最具影响力的工业设计企业之一。并多次得到国际权威专业设计奖项的认可，连续收获 设计奥斯卡德国红点、美国IDEA、日本Good Design Award等奖项。

路科公司运用成熟的工业设计平台，在2015年创办创易家品牌战略公司，以独创的品牌体系化服务模式，逐步的把从品牌 服务-产品系列化服务-生产产业链服务合并到一起。并且成功帮助部分中小型企业转型品牌化企业。同时广泛服务于联想、 SIEMENS、美的、BYD、格力、海尔、创维、腾讯等世界知名企业。凭借自身体系化的服务模式，现以为消费电子、医疗 器械、工业设备、智能终端和智能教育等领域取得了丰富的经验以及众多成功案例。

Shenzhen LOOK Creative Design Co.,Ltd was founded in 2011 , belongs to Shenzhen Leadel Group(one of ShenZhen BaoAn the 100 important private enterprises) It has become one of China's most influential industrial design companies after 5 years journey We have achived lots of International and professional Design awards continuously, Such as Oscar German Red Dot Design , USA IDEA, Japan Good Design Award etc other authorized International awards.

With the mature platform of industrial design ,the E-Best brand strategy companyhas established in 2015 successfully . It gradually has combined with Brand Service-Products Serialization Service-Production Chain Service together as unique brand system service mode. What’s more, we helped some small and medium enterprises have transferred into branded enterprises successfully.At the same time, wide range services in Lenovo,SIEMENS, Midea, BYD, Gree, Haier, Skyworth, Tencent and other world-renowned enterprises.With ourself unique system service mode,rich experience and numerous successful cases achieved in Consumer Electronics ,Medical Instruments,Industrial Equipment,Intelligent Terminals and Intelligent Education etc Filds.
